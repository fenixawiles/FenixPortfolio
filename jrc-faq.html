Frequently Asked Questions

Is TJRC peer-reviewed?

Yes. All submissions undergo double-blind peer review by qualified scholars with expertise in recursive methods, AI integration, or qualitative inquiry.

Do I need to be affiliated with an institution to submit?

No. We actively encourage submissions from independent researchers, early-career scholars, and unaffiliated thinkers.

What is recursive cognition?

Recursive cognition is a framework for thinking with, through, and about one’s own thought processes — often mediated by AI. It combines reflexivity, iteration, and co-construction as method and philosophy.

What are the publication fees?

There are none. TJRC is entirely open access and free to publish in. We believe knowledge should not be locked behind a paywall — either for readers or authors.

How can I become a reviewer or join the editorial board?

Email us at recursivecognitionjournal@gmail.com with a brief bio, CV, and a few lines on your interest in recursive inquiry.
