Submissions

We will eventually be accepting manuscripts that explore recursive dialogue, AI-mediated inquiry, and experimental research methodologies. We welcome both traditional submissions and boundary-pushing hybrid formats that blend theory, data, and narrative.

What We’re Looking For

  • Projects that treat AI as a co-thinker, not just a tool
  • Work rooted in recursive cognition, narrative inquiry, or reflexive process
  • First-time authors or early-career researchers experimenting with method
  • Meta-level reflections on authorship, agency, knowledge production, or AI ethics

Submission Process

Submissions are handled via our external portal. Please ensure your manuscript aligns with the journal’s mission and formatting guidelines. There are no submission fees. Accepted works will be published under a Creative Commons CC-BY 4.0 license.

If you have questions or wish to pitch an idea before submitting, feel free to contact us.
